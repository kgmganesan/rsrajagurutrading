About RSRAJAGURU TRADING

A dedicated coconut trading company based in Tirupur, Tamil Nadu, building a strong bridge between genuine farmers and reliable bulk buyers across India.

Our Focus

We specialise in buying fresh mature coconuts directly from farmers, farmer groups, and local mandis in key coconut-growing locations.

Our clients include wholesale traders, oil mills, copra makers, desiccated coconut units, supermarkets and export houses.
